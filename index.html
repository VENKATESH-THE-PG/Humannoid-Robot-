Humanoid Robot Moving with Our Gesture by Training AI Plus AI Assistant

About the Project

This project focuses on creating a humanoid robot that can respond to human gestures through AI training. The robot is designed to recognize and mimic human movements, providing an interactive experience.

Future Use

The humanoid robot can be utilized in various fields such as education, healthcare, and entertainment, enhancing human-robot interaction and supporting tasks in daily life.
